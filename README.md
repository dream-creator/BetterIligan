# BetterIliganCity.org 🌊

**BetterIliganCity.org** is a volunteer-driven, open-source initiative to modernize and simplify access to government services, public data, and local resources for the **City of Iligan**, Northern Mindanao, Philippines. 

Inspired by the civic tech movement, we are building a community-run digital portal for Iliganons, by Iliganons.

## 💡 Why This Was Made

Navigating local government procedures and finding reliable public information can sometimes feel like being lost in a digital bureaucracy. Information is often fragmented across different social media pages, outdated websites, or physical bulletin boards. 

BetterIligan was created to solve this by providing a single, clean, and accessible hub. We believe that accessing public services, discovering local heritage, and staying informed should be straightforward, fast, and user-friendly.
